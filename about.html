ABOUT

THE BEST SURF LESSONS IN LA SINCE 1990

Founded in 2003, Rapturecamps is a network of surfcamps in three countries on three different continents. We have establishments in Bali, Portugal and Nicaragua, all providing personalised surf holidays that blend adventure, comfort and style. Offering surf lessons, surf guiding and well-appointed accommodations in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere, all of our locations provide uniquely personal experiences that set us apart from larger resorts and package holidays.

Whether you’re an experienced surfer looking for new breaks in a new place or an absolute beginner, you’re wholeheartedly invited to embark on the surf adventure of a lifetime — in Bali, Portugal, Nicaragua or all three! Contact us about deals for groups of friends, family surf holidays and individuals.

About Our Founder

WITH OVER 25 YEARS OF SURFING EXPERIENCE, Christian is the owner and founder of LA Surfing School, California.

He grew up in Florida before relocating to Hawaii for much of his youth. He quickly gained competitive success and was a sponsored surfer for many years. He found his true passion was sharing his love of surfing with others. As he has travelled all over the world to surf, he has a vast knowledge base to share.

In addition to being an accomplished surfer, Christian is an excellent and patient teacher who can adapt his methods to your individual needs.

He has over ten years of experience teaching all levels of surfers. The majority of his experience came from working with the infamous “Uncle Bryan” and the Sunset Suratt Surf School on the North Shore of Oahu.
